Clicking the buttons above will do the following:
People:   Highlight person names.
Places:   Highlight place names.
Adds:   Highlight items marked 'add.' (Excluding: pencil additions)
Pencil:   Highlight additions done in pencil.
Gaps:   Toggles the display of textual gaps. (mouseover for details)
Notes:   Toggles the display of editorial notes. (mouseover for content)
Sources: Source material links are the dates in this color.
Unclear: Words with a dashed underline are unclear in the source text.
Tooltips: Words with a border will display related information if you hover your mouse cursor over them.
